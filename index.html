About Me

I am Dr. Rahul G. Sonkamble, Assistant Professor (CSE — AI&ML) at Pimpri Chinchwad University, Pune. I have 11+ years of academic experience. I hold a Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering with a focus on Privacy-Preserving Interoperable Blockchain for Electronic Health Records (EHR). My research expertise spans Blockchain, Zero-Knowledge Proofs (ZKPs), Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Cybersecurity, with 25+ publications in reputed journals including Nature Scientific Reports, IEEE Access, MDPI, and MethodsX. Beyond research, I actively foster industry–academia collaborations, enabling 27+ student internships through MoUs with companies like Klyntra, dHealth Network, Hypermine, and Accelchain. As a mentor and coordinator, I have successfully guided students in projects, workshops, and international symposiums, while also leading initiatives under IEEE and ACM Student Chapters. My vision integrates cutting-edge blockchain and AI applications with academic innovation, ensuring impactful contributions to research, teaching, and industry.
